Social Media Preparation

  • Make sure that you are only connected to people who you can trust.

  • Make sure that you do not have any inappropriate information in your status e.g. private information about yourself, the last party attended etc.

  • Make sure that your language is clear and grammatically correct e.g. check your spellings and read over anything before you post it online.

  • Make sure that you disconnect from anyone that you are not on good terms with.

  • Make sure that you only have like a passport picture of yourself e.g. not a full length picture of you a party drinking.

  • Make sure that you do not have pictures of others in your public area e.g. for family pictures, keep in your restricted area for family only.

  • Do not post your address or date of birth on your social media status.

  • Make sure that you update your profile from time to time e.g. just finished a new course, learnt a new skill, looking for work in a new area etc.

  • Make sure that you connect to others in your social media network and engage with them professionally e.g. accept invites for networking events.

  • Keep in touch with posts from those that you are connected to e.g. check to see if any new job vacancies have been advertised and try and connect with them.

  • Keep your language professional at all times e.g. do use rude language.

  • Be careful what you discuss about e.g. previous employers or a disagreement.
